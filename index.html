ABOUT

Hi, I’m Diana Chalabian, a passionate UX/UI designer and 3D artist based in Lahti, Finland. Currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in UX/UI Design at LAB University of Applied Sciences, I specialize in blending creativity with technical expertise to create meaningful and interactive user experiences. With a strong foundation in 3D modeling, jewelry design, and product visualization, I thrive on projects that combine artistry with technology. My journey includes exploring innovative fields like virtual reality (VR) and IoT technologies, with a focus on accessibility and mental health solutions. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me experimenting with oil painting, appreciating fine art, or working on prototypes that merge functionality with aesthetics. I’m always eager to learn, collaborate, and create designs that leave a lasting impact. Let’s craft something extraordinary together!
